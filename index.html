Welcome to the Simon game!

This game is about to train your memory.

Switch it on, click on the start button, listen the fields which had been pressed, and repeate them.

If you repeate it in the right order, a new field will be added, but if you make a mistake, the last row will be shown you, and you can try it again.

If you click on strict mode, every mistake will reset the current game. Your task is to reach 20 steps.
